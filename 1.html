Average Utilization*

This % value shows average utilization for your active VMs, based on the data points available. This is also referred to as the Mean value.

Standard Deviation (SD)**

This depicts the spread/range of utilization data for the VM. It shows how high and low your utilization is deviating from the average. E.g. If your average utilization is 10% and standard deviation is 3%, then ~68% of values lie between 7% and 13% (1 SD from the average). Similarly, ~95% of values lie between 4% and 16% (2 SD). We are estimating standard deviation value as (Max Utilization Value - Min Utilization value) / 4, across all data points available.

95th Percentile Value^

This depicts the 95th percentile of the observation. We are calculating it as Average + 2*SD.

Unutilized Capacity***

This depicts the amount of resources not used by the VM. It is calculated as (Total resources allocated – 95th percentile value). You can reduce the size of the VM by this utilized capacity by selecting a lower flavour. This will help you save on cost and resources.
